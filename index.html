Get the app

Install it on your iPhone

Good Morning runs as a web app installed directly to your home screen — no App Store, no account needed. It takes about thirty seconds.

1
Open this page in Safari It must be Safari — Chrome and other browsers won't support the installation.
2
Tap the Share button The box-with-arrow icon at the bottom of the Safari toolbar.
3
Tap "Add to Home Screen" Scroll down in the share sheet if you don't see it immediately.
4
Tap "Add" and open from your home screen The app opens full screen, exactly like a native app.

Your feedback matters

Making it count

This is an early prototype — things will be rough around the edges and that's expected. The most useful feedback at this stage is honest and specific: what felt confusing, what felt satisfying, what you wished the app did differently. As feedback is collected, the app will periodically update with changes.

You can submit feedback directly inside the app — scroll to the bottom of the Settings screen (the gear icon in the top corner) and you'll find a feedback field. There's no account needed and all feedback is anonymous.

Good Morning is a work in progress. Things may shift between visits as the app evolves. Your feedback is part of what drives those changes — try planning your tomorrow today and share what you notice.
