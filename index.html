Get the app

Install it on your iPhone

Good Morning runs as a web app installed directly to your home screen — no App Store, no account needed. It takes about thirty seconds.

1
Open this page in Safari It must be Safari — Chrome and other browsers won't support the installation.
2
Tap the Share button The box-with-arrow icon at the bottom of the Safari toolbar.
3
Tap "Add to Home Screen" Scroll down in the share sheet if you don't see it immediately.
4
Tap "Add" and open from your home screen The app opens full screen, exactly like a native app.

Before you start

Three things to know

The app is designed to feel intuitive, but these interactions aren't immediately obvious from looking at the screen.

←
Swipe left to complete a task A deliberate swipe across the task card marks it done and clears it from your schedule.
→
Swipe right to push a task Pushes the task to tomorrow without completing or deleting it. No judgment.
⬤
Long press for details and options Hold your finger on any task to open its detail view, edit it, pin it, or delete it.
☰
Drag handle to reorder Untimed tasks have a three-line handle on the right side of the card — press and hold it to drag tasks into your preferred order.

Your feedback matters

What we're looking for

This is an early prototype — things will be rough around the edges and that's expected. The most useful feedback at this stage is honest and specific: what felt confusing, what felt satisfying, what you wished the app did differently.

You can submit feedback directly inside the app — scroll to the bottom of the Settings screen (the gear icon in the top corner) and you'll find a feedback field. There's no account needed and all feedback is anonymous.

Not sure what to test? Try building tomorrow's schedule tonight — add a mix of daily tasks and a long-term project, then come back in the morning and see how it feels to wake up with your day already planned.
